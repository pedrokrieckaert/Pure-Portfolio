Competencies

Categories for evaluating the project.

For this project I have to demonstrate my mastery of the professional competencies of the User Experience Design program. Therefore - a practice based assignment/project - should adhere to the competencies below, to be approved by the university as a valid graduation project.

Research, Interpret and Define

Conduct research within a design process to explore:
User needs
Business requirements
Context of use
Technical opportunities
Interpret the findings and draw conclusions - identify design principles and challenges.

Create Concepts

Create a concept in response to a design challenge utilizing - user centric - design methods and creativity to come up with a range of ideas.

Visualize and Prototype

Create visualizations and artefacts during the process of generating and communicating ideas. Create prototypes that communicate the appearance, functionality, behaviour and user interaction - of the ideas.

Evaluate and Validate

Test, Evaluate, Validate designs and prototypes to gain insight into user interactions and experiences and the way the user needs and design goals are achieved.

Plan and Collaborate

Plan individual, team work and collaborate in teams, organizations, networks and communities. To get a sense of one’s role in a design team or project, and communicate effectively and convincingly while showcasing work and ideas in an inspiring way.

Reflect

Reflect on my work, attitude and account for ethical, cultural, and societal implications of my work. Reflect on technology and society, and their relation.

Project not a fit?

Potential project doesn't fit the competencies?

Don’t worry and reach out regardless, the project does not have to fit the competencies to the letter. So long as the category titles are available to meet during the project.
If a potential project is missing competencies - I am able to mediate a discussion with a mentor to discover any adjustments to account for the missing competencies.
